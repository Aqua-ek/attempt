About Private Groups

Private groups provide a secure, invitation-only space for focused collaboration. Members need a special key to join, ensuring your study sessions remain exclusive to selected peers.

Secure Access

Share the group key only with trusted members to maintain privacy.

Controlled Membership

You decide who can join your study group by distributing the access key.

Focused Discussions

Private groups enable more targeted conversations without outside distractions.

Best Practices

  • Choose a memorable but secure group key
  • Share the key through secure channels
  • Regenerate the key if compromised
  • Clearly communicate group expectations
